Hello there!
I'm Rebecca Van Aken

I'm a UX designer and front-end web developer with a strong passion for clean, accessible design, effective UX writing, and responsive web development. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Feel free to take a look around!

UX Writing

In my 5 years working in QA, designs with inefficient or confusing wording had me considering the user well-before entering the design world. This is how we communicate with our end users, so we must do so effectively.

Accessibility

As a neurodivergent designer, accessibility concerns will always take a front-seat when prioritizing design tasks and issues. When we design universally, we design for everybody, so it should be a top priority.

Web Development

I have experience with HTML, CSS, and a little bit of Javascript. I'm familiar with website platforms like Wordpress and Squarespace, but my preference will always be to code from scratch.
